Our redesign of Missourian

Lihua Yu; Yizhu Wang; Moqiu Ma

The color system contains two parts: the news-style color set and the lively one containing light red, blue and green. We combine them together to try to target the young readers, and also keep consistent with the purpose of the website of serious news.

For the homepage, the style of the title is inspired by physical newspapers, with necessary information for a local newspaper containing weather and date. The navigation bar is sticky. The title part is dark blue. We organize all articles into boxes. This style is more flexible to adjust according to the length of texts and the size of photos.

We have three sections: local, sports and opinion. For the local page, we heritage the style of boxes in the homepage. The number of articles can be unlimited. The right sidebar is for live Twitter feed, as Missourian uses social media as an important news publishing tool, especially for breaking news. For the sports part, the right sidebar is a schedule of games, based on a "table". For the opinion section, we add a brief introduction of the writers, which the readers may care about.

The two article pages contain many multimedia contents to diverse the texts. A photo gallery is added for the sports article because sports photos are usually compelling and attractive to readers.

The font style aims at showing contents clearly but not boring. There are six different font styles in article titles, text contents and the big title of "MISSOURIAN".
